ABOUT US

Grootwood is the wood trading company in Estonia. Our main activity is export of round logs. The company is active in more than 30 markets located mainly in the Far East, the Middle East, South America and Europe. Grootwood is a reliable company with a highly professional team. Once contacting our company, customers become our regular clients. We have known our partners and producers for decades, with whom we have a trusting work relationship.
